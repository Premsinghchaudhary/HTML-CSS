Basically, Vakratunda was the Ganesha who slayed the demon Matsarasura, and his vehicle is the lion on which
he is seated. Matsarasura was and still is a symbol of jealousy. Thus symbolically, Ganesha/Vakratunda is the
god who destroys and overcomes the destructive power of jealousy.
